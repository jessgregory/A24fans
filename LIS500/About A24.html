About A24


On the coast opposite Hollywood, directors Daniel Katz, David Fenkel and John Hodges began A24 Films, an independent film production and distribution company in New York City. A cult following defines the fanbase of A24 Film, primarily due to the themes of humanity in every plotline and the bold colors/styling of the setting and characters. A24 released five films in their first year, two of which were directed by Roman and Sofia Coppola. Since then, A24 Films has attracted the attention of world famous directors and acting talent to be part of their artistic output.
