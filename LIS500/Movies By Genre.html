About A24 Movie Genres Upcoming Releases My Ratings Participate

Popular Genres


Horror

Hereditary (2018): A high grossing film and popular horror film, Hereditary is considered the gateway film that entices people to watch more of A24’s work. A cursed family is haunted by its past, ultimately resulting in everyone being plagued by paranormal activity.


Midsommar (2019): Florence Pugh plays Dani, a young woman who experiences an unexpected tragedy and accompanies her boyfriend to Sweden. There, they are a part of a cult-y retreat that becomes a competition to the death.


False Positive (2021): Ilana Glazer stars as a pregnant woman named Lucy who’s expecting triplets (while Glazer is pregnant in real life). Lucy’s journey with her gynecologist throughout the pregnancy turns scary and violating.


Comedy

Eighth Grade (2018): Anxious eighth grader Kayla starts documenting her life through vlogs but becomes obsessed with how she’s being perceived on social media. Director Bo Burnham sought to accurately portray GenZ and their relationship with social media so he cast real eighth graders to play the characters.


The Disaster Artist (2017): James Franco is cast as famous Hollywood wannabe director Tommy Wiseau to comically replicate Wiseau’s journey creating film “The Room.”


The Bling Ring (2013): Based on a true story, a group of teenagers break into famous celebrity homes and rob them.

Drama

Uncut Gems (2019): Adam Sandler plays a jeweler with a serious sports gambling problem. His addiction gets him into trouble and in need of money, so he attempts to retrieve a precious gem. In a last attempt to win the money back, he places a very high stakes bet on a Boston Celtics game.


Minari (2021): A Korean family moves to rural Arkansas in 1980 to farm. The family endures struggles as they try to make it on their own, running into issues in their marriage, retaining employment and health.


Hot Summer Nights (2018): Timothee Chalamet stars as a teenager, Daniel, who lives in a beach town for the summer and starts selling drugs. Daniel falls for a girl he shouldn’t and avoids consequence to the best of his ability as he lives out his delinquent vacation.


Moonlight (2016): Chiron is a Black man growing up in the 1980s. The film takes us through his life as an adolescent through his young adult years, exploring his new independence and sexual discovery.

Coming-of-Age

Mid90s (2018): Directed by comedy legend Jonah Hill, the real-life California skate crew Illegal Civ is cast to depict a loyal group of teen boys navigating a captivating and sometimes dangerous subculture. The story follows a young boy, Stevie, who discovers his love for skating and uses it to escape his dysfunctional homelife. He then meets a cool group of mentors to teach him skate tricks and how to fit in.


Lady Bird (2017): Saorise Ronan stars as a highschool girl who goes by the name Ladybird. More than anything, Ladybird wants to leave her rural town for New York to become a famous writer. Much to her dismay, for now, she just has to finish high school and get along with her parents.
